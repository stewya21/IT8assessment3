Group Reflection


What went Well?

The group worked very well together and was able to come together to create a common goal. There was also a strong positive vibe across the team which made working together very easy. As a team, we definitely were able to achieve a great starting point for our project as well and actually lay some great groundwork for future development.

What could be improved?

Working remotely created some issues. We had multiple timezones and outside schedules to contend with. We also had some time constraints to deal with as most of us work full time.

At least one thing that was suprising?

Something that was suprising was seeing how everyone jumped in to try to learn or solve a problem even if they didn't necessarily have the skills. There was definitely an inclusive and safe environment to try new things and make mistakes.

At least one thing that you have learned about groups?

Working remotely across Australia is difficult and you really have to plan well to maximise everyone's time.
